BLuR - Blue Light "ur" Remover

== Description ==

This is one really simple plugin which will help you to retain your visitors attention on your stories.
When the plugin is activated it adds a simple filter that prevents the blue light from the screen, by this it makes your stories easier to read in the late hours of the day.<br />
<br />
<strong>BLuR</strong> is great for <i>News, Blogs</i> & for everyone who has what to say!<br />
<br />
Once the plugin is activated it will add this filter automatically to all blog post pages.
